Recommendation List

A list of classic reading materials for other journeymen who are interested in the same specialist areas in law and computer science as mine. I have a particular interest in the following areas:

Computer Science: Information Visualisation, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence in Games

Law: Public Law, Corporate Law, Corporate Governance
